Techblocks

Skill development plays a pivotal role in shaping a student’s academic growth and professional future. Recognizing this ,The IEEE student branch of our college organised Techblocks 10.1, a five day workshop from 16 September 2025 to 20 September 2025, aimed at enhancing technical expertise in four specialised domains: C++ , Chip Designing , Web Development and Gen AI.
